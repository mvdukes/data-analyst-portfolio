 SQL Sales Data Analysis

**Tools:** MySQL, Excel, PowerBI

**Goal:**
Analyze company sales by product line, region, and month using SQL queries then visualize that data in Excel and PowerBI

**Steps:**
1. Imported `sales_data_sample.csv` into MySQL.
2. Fixed date format using STR_TO_DATE.
3. Wrote queries to find:
	- Total revenue by product and country
	- The Top 5 countries by revenue
	- Monthly revenue trends
	- Best-selling product line
	- Average order value by deal size
4. Exported results as CSVs and visualized in Excel and Power BI.

**Skills Demonstrated:**
- SQL data import and cleaning
- Aggregation and grouping with SUM and GROUP BY
- Exporting and reporting query results

**Insights:**
	- Classic Cars brought the highest sales totaling $3,919,615.
	- The US accounted for over 30% of all revenue.
	- The top five countries by revenue were the US, Spain, France, and the UK. 
		-The US brought in over 3.6 million in revenue while the combined remaining 4 only brought in 3.4 million combined.
